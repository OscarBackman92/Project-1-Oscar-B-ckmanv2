About Us

Introduction

In 2018, my wife and I met on a blind date where we each ate a waffle at a spring market. Since that day we live together and waffles in all shapes and sizes have become our shared passion. However, we think that there is a shortage of good waffle places in Stockholm, especially in Jakobsberg. The Waffle truck is something that Stockholm has been missing!

Waffle History

Waffles used to be considered expensive, but not because of the waffle batter because it wasn't unusual eggs, extra fine flour or the first spring milk that made the waffles special. The precious thing was instead the waffle iron itself – often a cast and forged iron construction, and thus reserved for the bourgeoisie and the food culture of the higher social classes. Our waffles are certainly not baked with iron in cast iron construction, but in modern Teflon irons, but they are just as good as before and we don't want to limit ourselves to only those social classes. With us, everyone should be able to buy a good waffle!

Mission

The Waffle Truck is a breakfast and brunch truck that suits everyone thanks to our wide menu with well-composed combinations of fantastic ingredients and innovative flavor combinations. Our goal is to always make the guest happy with a combination of good food, accessibility, proximity & pleasant treatment. Together with personal efforts we achieve the guests' expectations in the best way. Welcome!
